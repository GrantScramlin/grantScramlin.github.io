General Services

Students receive one-on-one virtual lessons via Zoom, tailored to their learning goals and current skill level.

What I Teach

  • Intro to Programming (Python, JavaScript, etc.)
  • Web Development (HTML, CSS, React)
  • Game Development (Unity or Pygame)
  • Algorithm Practice & Coding Challenges
  • Project Guidance & Mentorship

Lesson Format

Each session is interactive and hands-on, with real-time screen sharing, code walkthroughs, and collaborative problem solving. Homework and small projects are assigned between lessons to reinforce learning.

Who This Is For

This is ideal for middle school, high school, or early college students who:

  • Want to learn programming from scratch
  • Need help with classwork or personal projects
  • Are preparing for internships, robotics competitions, or coding interviews

Added Benefits

Students who are enrolled in regularly scheduled lessons also receive free app publishing to Google Play, free web hosting on our dedicated web servers, and the ability to share their creations on our website.
